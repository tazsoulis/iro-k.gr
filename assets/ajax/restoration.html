The building's typological and morphological characteristics have unquestionable architectural and structural value, also it is a multi-story stone structure, relatively rare for the city of Thessaloniki. Today, the building presents significant damage to major destruction of the wooden frame inside and shelter, because of recent fire. However, the stone masonry remains upright highlighting the quality of construction.
