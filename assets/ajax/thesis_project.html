Revealing the hidden public space interventions of urban design in Karakasi street

students: Ioannidi Anastasia, Koutsioumpa Iro-Agoritsa

supervisors: Athanasiou E., Gerolympou A.

The study area is located in the 5th municipal department of Thessaloniki, is consisted of two major roadways, Karakasi and Solonos, and it is bounded by Kostantinou Karamanli, Kritis, Martiou and Sundika. The area occupies 38ha and includes important spaces, such as the two squares, Kaoudi and Kritis, and Galaxia, an open space which is used as parking and sports area.
